Valuation-led art marketplace

Art as culture. Art as market infrastructure.

Arthentico-X is a curated marketplace for whole artworks that combines premium discovery, valuation transparency, primary sales, and collector resale. The model is built for Austria first, then DACH, then Europe, with public catalogue access and premium subscription analytics layered on top.

What is being built

Product thesis

Arthentico-X should behave less like a generic online gallery and more like a transparent asset platform for art, where value signals, provenance, artist context, and resale liquidity are visible and legible to the buyer.

Discovery Curated catalogue with artist, medium, pricing, and market-context filters.
Transparency Public price, valuation range, methodology summary, and trend history.
Trust Provenance, certificates, seller type, contracting party, and shipping disclosures.
Liquidity Buyers can relist owned works while preserving the ownership trail.
Why it matters

Strategic wedge

The differentiation is not just “buy art online.” It is valuation clarity, reusable provenance, seller/collector coexistence, and a more investment-literate presentation of art without turning the product into a regulated securities platform in phase one.

For collectors Understand how a work is priced and how the artist’s market is moving.
For sellers Publish, track, and monetize inventory with more market context than a traditional gallery page.
For partners Galleries and auctions can onboard multiple artists and manage inventory in bulk.
For the platform Analytics subscriptions stack alongside take-rate revenue and future services.
Primary personas

Who the platform serves

Buyer / Collector

Browses, buys, tracks value, subscribes for deeper analytics, and later relists.

  • Portfolio, watchlist, alerts, orders
  • Artwork and artist trend charts
  • Shipping visibility before checkout

Artist

Builds a profile, lists works, tracks sales, and benefits from richer artist-market context.

  • Bio, exhibitions, prizes, grants
  • Inventory, payouts, and inquiries
  • Valuation inputs and performance

Gallery / Auction

Manages many artists under one organization, often with bulk operations.

  • Bulk upload and listing workflows
  • Sold portfolio and settlement reporting
  • Persistent artist attribution per work

Admin

Maintains trust, compliance, moderation, and payout correctness.

  • User and organization approvals
  • Authenticity, condition, and document review
  • Valuation overrides, disputes, subscriptions
Revenue model

Monetization

Commission

Marketplace fee on completed sales in both primary and secondary flows.

  • Primary target: 10% to 15%
  • Secondary target: 8% to 10%
  • Partner volume deals later

Subscriptions

Public discovery stays free. Deeper analytics and workflows are gated.

  • Collector: EUR 19/month
  • Professional: EUR 149/month
  • Enterprise: negotiated services

Expansion

Later layers can include concierge, appraisal, featured placement, and private-sale services.

  • Concierge workflows
  • Appraisal services
  • Partner-level packages
Scope discipline

Phase-one boundaries

In scope

  • Public marketplace and bilingual catalogue
  • Buyer, artist, and organization accounts
  • Valuation snapshots and charts
  • Fixed price, offer, inquiry, resale
  • Admin moderation and bulk import

Not in v1

  • Regulated fractional ownership
  • Platform custody and storage
  • Complex auction engine at launch
  • Multi-currency settlement
  • Escrow-by-default delivery model

Operating assumptions

  • Whole-artwork marketplace only
  • Self-serve onboarding with approval
  • Seller-managed or platform-managed shipping
  • Seller price separate from valuation estimate
  • Platform review based on photos and docs
Roadmap logic

Sequencing

Phase 1 Austria launch

Valuation-led whole-artwork marketplace with public catalogue, subscriptions, offers, direct checkout, and collector resale.

Phase 2 DACH / Europe expansion

Timed auctions, invite-only private sale flows, and deeper partner operations once supply and demand mature.

Future option Fractional layer

Only after traction, revenue, and dedicated legal/regulatory design for investment-like ownership models.
